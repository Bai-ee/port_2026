## Client Dashboard — Onboard in 30 Seconds

Enter your website URL to access automation tools and download a Client Brief based on your current website, brand identity, and visibility.

**Modular card pipeline:**

1. **Multi-Device View** — Desktop, tablet, and mobile screenshots composited into a device-frame mockup.
2. **Social Preview** — Parses metadata (title, description, OG image, favicon, canonical). Flags missing fields.
3. **Brand Snapshot** — Extracts colors, typography, layout, and motion from your live site via AI analysis.
4. **SEO + AI Visibility** — Runs PageSpeed Insights and an AI SEO audit in parallel. Scores LCP, CLS, TTFB, INP, and GEO citation readiness.
